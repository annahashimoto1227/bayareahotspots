About Us

Over the past few years, there has been a huge rise in food trends that have spread among young teens and adults. Accounts on social media platforms such as Instagram have huge followings of around five hundred thousand followers per account. However, they commonly feature cafes or shops in large cities such as Los Angeles or New York City. Consequently, people in the Bay Area have a harder time finding good places to eat. At Bay Eats, we have compiled data from various sources to make them easily accesible to anyone.

If you have any comments or suggestions, please contact us at bayeats@gmail.com!
