About Us

It has been a rising trend for young adults to meet up to eat and post about it on social media. Twitter threads of food hotspots and Intagram food accounts have huge followings. However, finding good places to try still require extensive research beforehand across multiple platforms such as Instagram, Twitter, Yelp, and Facebook. At Bay Eats, we have compiled data from various sources
to make finding highly rated food and drink places easy to access.
